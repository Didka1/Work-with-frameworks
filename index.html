About

Who we are?

We like to do some sport and we love to travel. That is why we decided to make a page about this and to help people to do sport for free and to organize interesting and not expensive trips.
We will share our experience and advice because we know that sometimes the good will is not enough and it is necessary to work and plan really hard.
Mission

Our dream?

Every member of our team was having problems with weight and BMI index in the past, so we know that it can be hard and we want to give support to everyone. Our dream is to show you that it can be very pleasant to do some sport. We hope that we can help your dreams to come true.
Travels are our hobby and way to cure our souls, so we will show you our pictures and share our emotions and ideas for easier travel.

Am I on the right place?

If you want to change something or just to try something new you can stay here for some time. Our editor Petar, strongly believes that one of the most important things in life is to be positive and never complain.
Actually three years ago he was 102 kilograms and really sad person, because he just had broken up with his girlfriend.

So, we made a bet with him, if he manage to lose 25 kilograms and go alone to a vacation in Paris, we will do a web page to support other people and he will be the editor...

He managed to do this and now you are at our page. Every day we are trying to be better than yesterday and now we can do this together.
